The Artist

Fred again..

Fred Again, stylized as Fred again.., born Frederik Gibson, is an English DJ, record producer, singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. Getting his early start in his career as a songwriter, he quickly saw massive career growth in 2018 when he co-wrote the song "Shotgun" sung by Geroge Ezra, reaching No.1 on the UK Singles Chart, remaining in the top 3 for 12 consecutive weeks. In the same year, Fred went on to co-write and produce songs for Rita Ora, Clean Bandit and Ed Sheeran.

Fred went on to create his first album titled Actual Life in 2019, using various audio clips from voice memos, social media and music by other artists to incoporate them into original tracks. Continuing to collaborate with other artists and appear in DJ sets worldwide, including Boiler Room, Fred was finally gaining more traction as a standalone artist in 2022. With the reach of TikTok and other social media platforms, Fred Again's music has reached millions.

The Genre

Dance/Electronic

Fred again.. creates Dance music but also plays the instruments he's putting on his tracks. Sampling various genres of music from different artists, Fred transforms any genre into upbeat, rhythmic tunes for anyone to dance along to.
